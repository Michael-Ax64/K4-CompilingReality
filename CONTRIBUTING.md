# Co-Inhabiting the Volume: How to Contribute

This repository is not a finished monument; it is a running engine. The initial thermodynamic labor of clearing the terminological debris and building the floor has been paid. The invitation now is to co-inhabit the volume. 

Because this framework models the Algebra of Four-Fold Distinction, contribution to it must also be four-fold. We do not accept arbitrary edits to the core text, but we actively invite structural contributions across all four faces of the topology. 

When you arrive, you must know which portal you are entering through.

## The Four Portals of Contribution

### 1. The U-Portal (Air / Logos / Structure)
**Who:** Software Engineers, Mathematicians, Logicians, Systems Architects.
**The Work:** You build the syntax and the tools. 
*   Expand the `/code/` and `/manifolds/` directories.
*   Build something practical for yourself. Integrate APIs, analyze something (feeds, posts, docs). Share.
*   Run the blinded permutation sweeps (`ProofS`) on novel, untested 4-element systems.
*   Formalize the projection operators linking the algebra to physical tensors.
**The Rule:** Your code must compile. Your math must close. 

### 2. The I-Portal (Water / Pathos / Relational Flow)
**Who:** Clinicians, Community Builders, Therapists, Translators, Writers.
**The Work:** You provide the therapeutic gap and the subjective translation. The math is contentless until it is bridged into lived experience.
*   Engage the notion presented here. Share your notes and write about your learning process.
*   Write new `*Subjective` registers and `TooTrue` mythopoetic diagnostics for specific industries or pathologies.
*   Apply the 12-facet diagnostic to active, lived human systems (people, organizations, relationships) to resolve plane-lock.
*   Translate the framework’s structures out of Indo-European packaging to verify its universality (`ProofS` Step 5).
**The Rule:** You must not sacrifice rigor for poetic comfort. Feelings and Intuitive/Spiritual overtones are fine, but ground them in the codex.

### 3. The P-Portal (Fire / Kairos / Initiation)
**Who:** Visionaries, Founders, Disruptors, Domain Experts.
**The Work:** You provide the raw, ungrounded anomalies. You bring the problems that your specific field cannot solve because it is trapped in a Kessler Cascade. 
*   Bring heavily plane-locked, unresolved domains and smash them against the 12 equations.
*   Force the framework into new territory. 
*   Drive the generator to produce new structural hypotheses.
**The Rule:** You must be willing to let the framework dismantle the favored nouns and "hidden variables" of your domain.

### 4. The R-Portal (Earth / Ethos / Ground)
**Who:** Funders, Infrastructure Providers, Resource Allocators.
**The Work:** You pay the Landauer Tax. 
In a volumetric system, money and resources are not "dirty" or "external" to the intellectual work. They are stored metabolic energy. They are thermodynamic fuel. 
*   Rent and Compute need to paid.
*   Evolving the `AlgebraicSwarmController` requires solid/secure funding.
*   Executing Step 5 of `ProofS` requires hiring human domain-natives across distant linguistic lineages.
*   Etc, etc.
None of this can happen purely in the abstract space of Air ($U$). It requires the material bedrock of Earth ($R$). A person who brings resources is offering to be the ground so the system can actualize. 
**The Rule:** You fund the infrastructure; you do not buy the right to alter the math.

## The Core is 'more or less sealed'

**Patches to the foundational algebra (L1-L3) are not accepted.** 
The dual-binary seed and the 12 equations are not up for debate. They are mathematically closed. If you submit a pull request trying to "tweak" the geometry because you prefer discussing 5-pole or 3-pole system at the moment, it will be rejected. 

If you believe the core math is wrong, you do not submit a patch. You submit a **Kill Condition**.

**The Falsifier:** The core claim is that this specific four-fold algebraic structure recurs across domains with its relationships intact. Find a domain where the four-fold pattern clearly appears but the algebraic relationships between the mapped poles *do not hold*—where the structure is present but the algebra breaks—and the central claim is broken. 

Bring that domain, trace the breakdown, and present the anomaly. A framework that asks others to engage its claims owes them its own kill-condition. This is ours.

Look before you rule. Run the generator. Step inside.
