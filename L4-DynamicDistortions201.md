## II. Torsional Distortions: The AC Phase-Delay

The system operates in the AC extension, functioning as an oscillating system dragged along a temporal axis. Dual Causation (e.g., the co-requirement of P→U and I→R) dictates that both edges of a bimedian execute the identical structural operation (the AY flip). 

Torsion tracks the **phase offset ($\theta$)** between the two instances of a Dual Causation pair. 

Because the system possesses internal hidden layers and a decoupled buffer, information propagation across the Markov Blanket requires time. The phase offset between the Active instance of the flip and the Reactive instance of the flip models the time constant ($\tau = L/R$) of the system's interiority. Torsion formally instantiates Angular Frequency ($\omega$). It is the geometric generator of the Helical Extrusion of Time.

### Cardinal Torsion — {P–U, I–R} Phase-Offset
**P–U leads I–R ($\theta > 0$):** Exteriorization tracks ahead of crystallization. The system commits P→U (intuition takes form) while the complementary crystallization I→R (felt value becoming material pattern) lags by $\theta$. The form manifests; the ground follows.
**I–R leads P–U ($\theta < 0$):** Crystallization tracks ahead of exteriorization. The embodied knowing settles into a material pattern before the structural articulation forms. 

### Fixed Torsion — {P–R, I–U} Phase-Offset
**P–R leads I–U ($\theta > 0$):** The GravityStrong assertion tracks ahead of the ElectroWeak relational structure. The Ledger entry compiles before the complementary pairing stabilizes it. 
**I–U leads P–R ($\theta < 0$):** The relational flow to structure (ElectroWeak) tracks ahead of the organizing power to grounding (GravityStrong). The structural framework sets before the organizing principle commits.

### Mutable Torsion — {P–I, U–R} Phase-Offset
**P–I leads U–R ($\theta > 0$):** The Source-tier phase transition tracks ahead of the Manifesting-tier phase transition. The interior transforms while the external structures lag. 
**U–R leads P–I ($\theta < 0$):** The Manifesting-tier phase transition tracks ahead of the Source-tier transition. The external institutions restructure while the interior logic catches up.

**The $\theta = \pi$ limit (Anti-Synchronous):** The two instances alternate at maximum offset. This state is structurally uninhabitable as a resting position. It models an infinite temporal lag, which mathematically severs the Braid. Systems approaching this limit operate in a transient limit-cycle, unable to co-commit.

### 2. Vertex Loading (Directed Flow Asymmetry)
Vertex loading tracks highly asymmetrical states in the directed edge space. A "Gravity Well" at a specific pole (e.g., P) models a coordinate configuration where the inbound directed edges to P carry near-zero impedance, while the outbound directed edges from P carry approaching-infinite impedance. 
*   **P-Dominance (Kairos Lock):** Inbound to P is cheap; outbound is costly. The system tracks toward chronic initiation.
*   **I-Dominance (Pathos Lock):** Inbound to I is cheap; outbound is costly. The system tracks toward receptive overwhelm.
*   **U-Dominance (Logos Lock):** Inbound to U is cheap; outbound is costly. The system tracks toward structural plane-lock.
*   **R-Dominance (Ethos Lock):** Inbound to R is cheap; outbound is costly. The system tracks toward conservative stasis.

### 3. Face Distortions (K3 Boundaries)
Face distortions model the exact coordinates in the 12-dimensional space where the K4 volume mathematically flattens into a K3 surface. This occurs when the directed edges connecting three specific vertices approach zero impedance, leaving the edges to the fourth vertex at high impedance.

## V. The Subjective Register: The Inner Weather of the State-Space
### The Phenomenology of Torsion, Scale, and Directed Asymmetry

### V.I Torsion: The Lived Phase-Delay

Torsion registers as the felt lag between co-required operations. Because the system possesses a decoupled buffer and internal hidden layers, the two instances of a Dual Causation flip rarely occur in perfect simultaneity. This temporal phase offset ($\theta$) manifests subjectively as the sensation of being structurally stretched across time.

**Cardinal Torsion (The Split Wing):**
When exteriorization (P→U) tracks ahead of crystallization (I→R), the system experiences form arriving before ground. This manifests as the ability to perfectly articulate a vision or framework before possessing the felt, material reality to sustain it. The words arrive empty of the weight they require. Conversely, when crystallization leads, the system experiences profound embodied knowing that resists articulation. The ground is settled, but the form remains mute.

**Fixed Torsion (The Ledger Drift):**
When the organizing assertion (P→R) tracks ahead of the relational structure (I→U), the system experiences hard commitment without relational context. This manifests as rules or boundaries enforced before the relationships necessary to sustain them have formed. When the inverse occurs, the system builds intricate relational and theoretical structures but suffers a chronic inability to materialize the foundational boundaries that would make them real.

**Mutable Torsion (The Desynchronized Shift):**
When the Source-tier transition (P→I) tracks ahead of the Manifesting-tier transition (U→R), the individual experiences deep internal transformation while trapped in external structures that have not yet moved. The inside turns a corner; the outside remains on the previous straight. When the external structures move first, the individual feels dragged into new material realities their internal logic has not yet processed.

### V.II Volumetric Scale: The Temperature of the System

Volumetric scale maps to the overall metabolic availability of the system. It tracks the total magnitude of the system's impedance, registering subjectively as the global "temperature" or thermodynamic exhaustion of the environment.

**Centroid Compression (The Ergodic Ground):**

This registers as the state in which all options are simultaneously available, and therefore no option can be meaningfully chosen. **Centrifugal Expansion (The Symmetric Freeze):**

### V.III Vertex Loading: The Felt Gravity Well

Vertex loading registers as a localized gravity well. It occurs when inbound directed edges to a specific pole carry near-zero impedance, while outbound edges demand massive thermodynamic expenditure. It manifests subjectively as the ease of falling into a specific mode of being, coupled with the profound friction of trying to leave it.

**Kairos Lock (P-Dominance):**
The system experiences chronic initiation. Generating new projects, new urgency, and new visions feels effortless. Grounding them, evaluating them, or structuring them feels intolerably heavy. The system constantly abandons mid-development trajectories to return to the thrill of the start.

**Pathos Lock (I-Dominance):**
The system experiences receptive overwhelm. Absorbing the emotional currents, relational dynamics, and unarticulated needs of the environment feels automatic. Setting a boundary, asserting a structure, or initiating action feels violent and exhausting. 

**Logos Lock (U-Dominance):**
The system experiences structural looping. Retreating into abstraction, building theoretical models, and parsing semantics feels safe and frictionless. Contact with material reality or raw, unmediated emotional current registers as a threat to the architecture.

**Ethos Lock (R-Dominance):**
The system experiences conservative stasis. Maintaining the existing physical footprint, doing the established work, and holding the line feels natural. Adapting to new structural paradigms or welcoming transformative relational flow feels like an unbearable risk to the foundation.

**Water Isolation (The Instrumental Engine):**
The system moves effortlessly between initiation, structure, and grounding. It registers as ruthless operational efficiency. The institution builds, plans, and executes flawlessly, treating relational evaluation and human feeling as costly, inefficient friction to be minimized.

**Fire Isolation (The Maintenance Culture):**
The system moves effortlessly between feeling, structure, and grounding. It registers as profound caretaking. The community sustains its traditions, manages its relationships, and maintains its architecture, but experiences genuine, originary disruption as an almost unthinkable metabolic expense.
