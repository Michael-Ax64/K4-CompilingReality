## I. The Geometric Stance and the Shielded Boundary

A bounded frame surrounded by other bounded frames must map their interiors without dissolving its own boundary. The Algebra of Four-Fold Distinction offers exactly one equation for that job — the Geometric Stance, homed in the Air/Logos quadrant:

$$U = \sqrt{P \cdot R}$$

And the equation's defining feature is what it leaves out: **its AbsentVar is Current/Pathos ($I$).** To model an interior, the relational current is *held in the buffer, not run live.*

Run $I$ active while modeling another and you invite intermodulation — the $P$–$I$ fusion the inventory names *GraviFlux / The SourceVoid*, in which initiating and receiving lose their distinction and the boundary between observer and observed dissolves. Hold $I$ absent and the blanket holds. The map stays a map instead of becoming a merger.

Now the tension that makes this precise, because a careless reading sinks the whole document. **You cannot see the other's $P$ or $R$.** The Ledger is interior; the dissipative ground is interior; the aperture is blind to both. So $U = \sqrt{PR}$ cannot be a *computation over measured quantities* — there are no measured quantities to compute over. It is a *constraint on the shape of the reader's map.* Whatever the other's drive and ground actually are, the reader's structural model must relate to them as their geometric mean. The equation does not deliver $P$ and $R$. It tells you the only lawful shape a blind map of them can take.

And here is the first thing the field has never been able to say, because it never had the equation:

**The geometric mean is symmetric. Swap $P$ and $R$ and $U$ does not move. The structural map of another mind is therefore constitutionally unable to tell drive from ground — disposition from situation. It sees only their product.**

The tendency to read another's behavior as *character* when it was *circumstance*, and to feel the reverse about oneself, is not a bias bolted onto an otherwise clean instrument. It is the instrument. 

The Geometric Stance is disposition–situation blind by construction, and separating the two requires a second operation the base stance does not contain. Social psychology spent fifty years cataloguing the fundamental attribution error as a quirk of motivated cognition. It is a property of the square root.

## II. Two Instances, One Stance

The stance scales invariantly. Running it requires a bounded reader, a reference oscillator, a motion-only aperture, and a linguistic bridge for content. What changes across instances is not the stance — it is the reference.

**The asymmetric instance (celestial).** A bounded human reads the phase-relations of the solar system. The reference carries an enormous inertial differential; its angular frequency is, for all purposes, absolute. Saturn does not re-time itself because you looked. Stability is a gift of the reference's mass.

**The symmetric instance (mind-reading).** A bounded human reads another bounded human. And here the diagnosis must be stated exactly, because the obvious version is wrong. The problem is *not* merely that the inertial differential is small. A metronome has near-zero inertial differential with you and makes a perfectly serviceable reference. The problem is that this reference **reads back.**

**The other mind re-tunes its own frequency in response to being read. You are not measuring an oscillator. You are measuring an oscillator that is measuring you.**

Two mutually-reading, mutually-adapting oscillators, each adjusting to the other's adjustments, do not settle. The naive protocol — the one that worked against the sky — enters a non-convergent chase and never phase-locks. Reactivity, not low mass, is what breaks it. This is why you can read a clock, a tide, or a market index more easily than a lover mid-argument: the clock is not revising itself around your attention.

$P$ is always what was spent, committed, dissipated, real. $Q$, in every guise, is what is held, deferred, stored, imaginary. A mind is a large $Q$. Interiority is a high $Q_\text{factor}$. Empathy is the deliberate spending-down of your own $Q$ to fund another's phase-lock. The letter was trying to tell us.
