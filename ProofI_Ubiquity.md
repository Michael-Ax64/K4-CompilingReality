## II. The First Leg: Ubiquity From Below

Every manifest interaction at atomic scale and above reduces to charge dynamics.

Chemical bonds are configurations of charge — the sharing or transfer of electrons between nuclei, held in specific geometric relationships by the balance of electromagnetic attraction and repulsion. Every molecule is a charge configuration. Every material property — conductivity, viscosity, transparency, hardness — is a consequence of how charge is arranged and how it responds to perturbation.

Metabolic processes are charge dynamics. ATP synthesis is a proton gradient driving charge across a membrane. Neural firing is the rapid redistribution of ion charges across a lipid bilayer. Photosynthesis is charge separation driven by photon absorption. The machinery of life, at every level of resolution, is charge in motion.

Gravitational effects at human scales are mediated electromagnetically: the reason you cannot fall through a floor is not gravity but the electromagnetic repulsion between electron clouds. Every tactile sensation, every structural support, every chemical reaction occurring under gravitational influence is, at the level of its actual mechanism, charge dynamics.

The strong nuclear force and the Higgs mechanism operate below atomic scale, where the framework explicitly does not claim jurisdiction. Gravity at cosmological scales operates differently. The claim is not reductionist totality — not "literally all physics is electricity." The claim is precisely bounded: charge-current-potential-resistance tracks the relational structure present at every scale of manifest interaction we can instrument, in every process we can observe directly, without exception at the atomic-and-above scale the framework addresses.

Identifying the local mechanism of a phenomenon does not falsify its global topology; it merely explains how that topology is instantiated in a specific substrate. Group 14 electron arithmetic is the specific mechanistic requirement for carbon's tetravalence. The fact that the arithmetic resolves to exactly four bonds is the physical execution of the K4 topology, not a refutation of it. A mechanism is how the structure pays its thermodynamic rent.

This means: when the corpus uses the DC algebraic framework, it is not reaching for the most vivid available analogy. It is naming the precise topological system that tracks every process the framework describes. The ohmic four-fold is not borrowed from electrical engineering. Electrical engineering is one domain in which humanity made the four-fold explicit. The four-fold was already operating in every chemical, biological, cognitive, and social process the framework addresses, before electrical engineering named it.

Two independent paths. One ascends from the substrate of manifest reality — from the fact that charge dynamics is what everything above atomic scale is made of.
