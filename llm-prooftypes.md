**Kill condition:**

## II. The ZPE Tension (Proof by Paradox)

**Form:** A contradiction is exhibited and proven to be *structural*. The system does not work *despite* the contradiction; it works *because* of it. 
**Instance:** `ProofC` (Crystal). The lattice is macroscopically committed and microscopically never at rest.
**Kill condition:**

If resolving the paradox destroys the system, it is a load-bearing pillar. If resolving the paradox leaves the system fully functional, it was a mistake or terminological debt.
