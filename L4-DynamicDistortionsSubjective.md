# K4 Dynamic Distortions: The Subjective Register
## What Distortion Feels Like From Inside

*This document is the experiential complement to L4-DynamicDistortions. Where that document inhabits the combinatorics — edge weights, Hamiltonian cycles, bimedian axes, attractor states — this document inhabits the phenomenology. What does each distortion feel like to a system undergoing it? What is the inner weather of a fused edge? What texture does each attractor have from inside?*

*Structure follows L4-DynamicDistortions. The same progression — setup, six edges, three axes, three attractors — but the register shifts from structural to experiential. Both documents are required to hold the full K4 interior.*

## I. The Regular Tetrahedron: The Felt Ground State

Before distortion, a calibration.

The regular tetrahedron — all six edges equally weighted, no fusion, no stretching — is not a neutral or empty state. It is the most demanding state available. Equal weight on all six edges means all six relationships are equally alive, equally available, equally costly to traverse. Nothing has been simplified by being made cheap. Nothing has been clarified by being made expensive. Every transition you might make carries the same full freight.

This is what `.behold()` feels like from inside. Not serenity — that misreads it. Not blankness. The felt quality of the regular tetrahedron is more like the moment before a committed move in a complex game, when all lines of play are still open and the full weight of possibility is present without any of it having been discharged. It hums. It does not rest.

The equal-weight ground state is also the most metabolically expensive configuration to maintain. To hold all six edges simultaneously active — to refuse the relief of fusing two poles, of letting one relationship become trivially cheap — costs something. The cost is the Reactive Power circulating in the buffer: energy that is genuinely present, genuinely doing something, but not appearing as output. The system under equal weighting is working hard to remain available.

What collapses this state is not failure. What collapses it is the arrival of a specific driving frequency — a pressure that is stronger along one axis than the others. Once that pressure exceeds the system's capacity to remain equidistant from all three attractors, it begins to drift. An edge begins to soften. The tetrahedron becomes irregular. And this is where the interesting phenomenology begins.

## II. What Fusion Means Experientially

When an edge weight moves toward zero — when two poles begin to fuse — what happens from inside is not the disappearance of one of them. Both poles are still structurally present. What disappears is the *cost of moving between them*. The transition that used to require a full commitment, a genuine expenditure of the Landauer Tax, becomes free. And free transitions are, in the system's experience, no transitions at all.

This is a precise observation. When crossing the P–U edge costs nothing, you can be at P and at U simultaneously — the traversal registers nothing. The two positions have collapsed toward a single position, not because either vertex has been removed but because the edge between them no longer has the thickness that made them genuinely distinct. They are distinct in principle but not in practice. The structure carries both labels; the dynamics honor neither distinction.

The experience of this is not the experience of having more. It is the experience of having something without the possibility of not having it — which means the thing can no longer be chosen. Choice requires a cost. When the edge is free, the relationship between those two poles can no longer be committed to because commitment requires something to commit *against*.

This is the general phenomenology of all six fusions. Each one removes a specific kind of commitment from the system's available repertoire. Each one is simultaneously a relief and a loss — a relief because that edge no longer needs to be paid for, a loss because the payment was what made the relationship real.

## III. The Cardinal Axis: Exteriorization and Crystallization Under Stress

*{P–U, I–R} — the Source-to-Manifesting edges — the passage from interior to exterior*

The Cardinal edges are the two passages by which the interior becomes exterior. Fire becomes Air: the originary impulse takes form, becomes articulable, crosses from the inside of the system to the outside where it can be received. Water becomes Earth: the felt evaluation solidifies into demonstrated character, becomes material, crosses from the flowing interior to the stable exterior where it can be trusted.

These two passages share a structural property: they are both Asserting/Yielding flips. The interior poles (Fire/Water) are the pure ones — both qualities aligned. The exterior poles (Air/Earth) are the mixed ones — qualities crossed. Moving from interior to exterior always involves taking on a quality that was not present before. The knot forms at this crossing. The gap between conception and expression is precisely the space this transition traverses.

When the Cardinal edges come under stress, it is the passage itself that distorts.

---

### Fuse P–U: *The Knot Collapses*

Fire and Air are normally separated by the full cost of exteriorization — the gap in which an interior impulse becomes an exterior form, in which the intuition becomes language, in which the Kairos becomes Logos. That gap is where translation happens. It is uncomfortable, often slow, always lossy. The idea you had is never quite the idea you expressed. Something is gained in the expression; something is also always left behind in the interior.

When P–U fuses, the knot collapses. The gap closes to zero. The impulse arrives already in its own words. The intuition IS the articulation.

The first register of this is unmistakable and feels like grace: the sentence that writes itself, the solution that arrives already in the form that can be handed to someone else, the creative moment in which there is no interval between conceiving and expressing. Artists and mathematicians report this as among the most prized of states. It feels like the system running without friction — not because the work is easy but because the work and the impulse to work have become one motion.

The second register is subtler and arrives later: the loss of the therapeutic gap. The gap between conceiving and expressing is not only where translation happens. It is where *checking* happens. The moment you catch the impulse that would have been wrong, the phrase that would have landed badly, the insight that would have been true for you but false for the situation. When P–U fuses, checking disappears. The impulse is the expression before it can be evaluated.

In human terms: the wound that speaks itself before you can choose whether to speak it. The conviction that cannot distinguish itself from the evidence for the conviction, because the conviction arrived already articulated, already with its own reasons. The prophetic certainty that is also, for that very reason, uncheckable.

The gift and the danger share the same mechanism.

---

### Fuse I–R: *The Setting*

Water and Earth are normally separated by the full cost of crystallization — the gap in which a felt evaluation becomes a stable pattern, in which the living flow of relational knowing becomes the demonstrated character that can be relied upon, in which caring becomes structure. That gap is where transformation happens. It is the space in which feeling becomes form without losing its feeling-quality, in which the care that flows becomes the care that holds.

When I–R fuses, the crystallization completes without remainder. The felt value IS the ground. The care IS the structure. There is no gap between what moves through you and what you are made of.

This is the texture of a very long love. Not the love that flows — not the early state where feeling is still clearly distinguishable from form — but the state after decades in which you can no longer find the boundary between caring about someone and being structured by them. They have become the shape of the room. Not metaphorically. Structurally. The way your attention orients, the things you notice, the forms your thinking takes — all of it has crystallized around the ongoing fact of that relationship until the crystallization and the current are one thing.

The beauty of I–R fusion is its depth. The knowledge that is no longer knowledge because it has become body. The ethic that is no longer a rule you follow but a shape you inhabit. The stability that comes not from deciding to be stable but from having become so thoroughly what you care about that the caring and the being cannot be separated.

The cost is the loss of the flowing quality. Water that has become Earth can no longer find its level in new containers. The crystallized care cannot easily reorient. The embodied knowing resists being updated precisely because it has lost its flowing character — it is not adaptive because it is not liquid. The settlement is permanent in ways that a living current would not be.

### Both Cardinal Edges Fused: *The Apeiric Field*

When both P–U and I–R collapse simultaneously, the entire Source-to-Manifesting passage is gone. Fire IS Air. Water IS Earth. Interior and exterior are no longer distinct kinds of territory.

This state is not empty. That is the first and most important thing to understand about it. The Apeiric Field is not the blank page. It is the too-full one. The system is generating — it is doing everything that Source poles do, which is to generate continuously — but nothing is crystallizing, because crystallization requires a traversal cost that no longer exists. The system is simultaneously all of what it might become and none of what it has become.

The phenomenological register of the Apeiric Field is familiar to anyone who has experienced creative saturation: the state in which you have too many ideas to have any idea, in which the generative capacity has outrun the capacity to commit, in which every possible direction is equally available and therefore equally unavailable. Not depression — the system is fully energized. Not blankness — the system is full. It is the specific suffering of maximum Apparent Power with zero Real Power: everything available, nothing converted.

In cosmological terms, this is the pre-differentiated plasma — the state before the knot forms, before Source touches Manifesting at a cost that generates a specific shape. The Apeiric Field is energetically rich and structurally indeterminate. It has not failed to generate. It is generating so completely that there is nothing to which a specific form could attach.

What it needs is not more energy. It needs a frequency — a specific driving rate that would make one traversal cost more than another, would re-stiffen one Cardinal edge, would let the knot form again. It needs the conditions for the interior to have an exterior that is genuinely distinct from it.

## IV. The Fixed Axis: Assertion and Yield Under Stress

*{P–R, I–U} — the AR-flip edges — where scale changes while character holds*

The Fixed edges are the two passages along which the system changes its scale — its Active/Reactive quality — while holding its character constant. P becomes R: asserting at the cosmic scale becomes asserting at the local scale, the organizing principle becomes the grounding principle, the top-down becomes the bottom-up, while the Asserting character is conserved throughout. I becomes U: yielding in the flowing register becomes yielding in the structuring register, Pathos becomes Logos, the evaluative current becomes the potential framework, while the Yielding character is conserved throughout.

These edges carry the system's information. DNA pairs along Fixed edges precisely because information storage requires holding one quality constant (Asserting or Yielding) while flipping the other (Active/Reactive). The Fixed bimedian is the axis along which the system encodes its own history in a form that can be read back.

When the Fixed edges come under stress, it is the distinction between scales that distorts — and with it, the system's capacity to hold information stably.

---

### Fuse P–R: *The Weight*

Fire and Earth are both Asserting, but their Asserting is normally of recognizable different kinds. P/Fire asserts at the largest scale — it organizes from above, curves the space that everything else moves through, initiates without being initiated. R/Earth asserts at the smallest scale — it grounds from below, holds what has arrived, confines what would otherwise scatter, provides the resistance that makes form possible.

When P–R fuses, the distinction between these two scales of assertion disappears. The gravity of the situation and the weight of the stone become the same thing. Cosmic organization and local confinement become indistinguishable.

The texture of this state is the texture of an institution so old that its organizational logic has become indistinguishable from its physical constraints. The rules are the walls. The procedures are the load-bearing structure. When you ask why a thing is done a certain way, the answer is not "because we decided this was best" — an Active assertion that could in principle be revised — but "because this is what we do," which is both Active (it's an organizing principle) and Reactive (it's a material fact about the floor plan). You cannot find the seam between the intention and the inertia. They have fused.

The experience of being inside P–R fusion is the experience of being unable to distinguish the gravity that organizes you from the gravity that holds you. Both feel identical from inside. You cannot tell whether the force shaping your trajectory is cosmological — operating from the level of fundamental structure — or hadronic — operating from the level of confinement that cannot be reasoned with. They feel the same. They have the same texture: Asserting, persistent, not interested in your preferences.

The loss is the ability to negotiate with the organizing principle by appealing to the grounding facts, or to negotiate with the grounding facts by appealing to the organizing principle. When they are fused, there is no daylight between them in which the negotiation could occur.

---

### Fuse I–U: *The Resonance*

Water and Air are both Yielding, but their Yielding is normally recognizably different in kind. I/Water yields in the flowing register — it evaluates relationally, carries felt knowing from place to place, finds its level, responds to the contours it encounters. U/Air yields in the structuring register — it mediates potential difference, holds a framework over the terrain and lets things move according to the gradient it establishes.

When I–U fuses, the Active/Reactive distinction in the Yielding domain disappears. The structural framework and the relational current become the same thing. The theory has become the therapy. The Logos IS the Pathos. The gradient and the flow that follows the gradient are one motion.

This is the state L5 calls resonance from inside: not when you have forced the system into alignment but when you have discovered that the rhythm you were tracing and the rhythm the system naturally wants to trace are the same rhythm. At this point, the question "is this the structure or is this my experience of the structure?" becomes unanswerable — and not because the answer is hidden, but because the distinction has genuinely collapsed.

There is a specific moment in deep analytic work, in long teaching relationships, in any sustained practice where understanding is the medium — when the model you are using to understand a thing has become so accurate, so continuously corrected by contact with the thing itself, that you can no longer find the seam between the model and the thing. You are not using a framework. You are inside a framework that is also the inside of the phenomenon. The map and the territory share a surface.

This is I–U fusion. It is the experiential correlate of the ElectroWeak unification: the Yielding domain running at resonance, the Active/Reactive distinction no longer energetically meaningful, the current and the potential operating as a single undifferentiated Yielding field.

The beauty is complete and the danger is identical. You cannot correct a model you cannot distinguish from reality. The therapeutic gap — the space between the framework and the life inside it — is where error can be caught before it compounds. When I–U fuses, that space is gone. The resonance is perfect and therefore unrevised. If it was right before the fusion, it will continue to be right. If it was subtly wrong before the fusion, the subtle wrongness is now invisible.

### Both Fixed Edges Fused: *The Isotropic Pole*

When both P–R and I–U collapse simultaneously, the Active/Reactive distinction is gone entirely — in both character registers.

The Isotropic Pole is not what freedom feels like. Freedom requires a cost structure — requires that some moves cost more than others, that choosing one path means not choosing others. The Isotropic Pole has removed the cost structure from the AR axis entirely. Every Active/Reactive pairing is equally cheap. G pairs with C for the same cost as G pairs with G. The Read-Only Ledger cannot write stably because stable writing requires the Fixed edges to be expensive — requires that the AR-flip have genuine discriminating power.

The felt quality of this state is the felt quality of a high fever in which everything connects to everything, in which every association is equally valid, in which the elaborate architecture of meaningful distinction — which usually feels like the furniture of the mind — has been rendered weightless. Not the absence of content. The presence of content that cannot be organized because organization requires that some connections cost more than others.

The Isotropic Pole is the pre-Higgs ground state at the level of experience. Maximum symmetry, which means: no information can be written. Every possible pairing is equally possible. Nothing means more than anything else along the AR axis, because the AR axis has no gradient.

What re-stiffens it — what performs the experiential analog of the Higgs mechanism — is the return of specificity. Not the imposition of a rule from outside, but the emergence of a temperature at which one pairing begins to cost slightly more than another, at which the equal-weighting of all Fixed-axis options begins to break, at which the system can again tell the difference between holding Asserting constant and holding Yielding constant. The restoration of the AR distinction is the restoration of the capacity for information. It is also, not coincidentally, the restoration of the capacity for memory.

## V. The Mutable Axis: The Diagonal Under Stress

*{P–I, U–R} — the both-flip edges — where both qualities change simultaneously*

The Mutable edges are the most violent transitions available in the K4 topology. Every other edge flips one bit. The Mutable edges flip both. P to I: Active flips to Reactive AND Asserting flips to Yielding simultaneously. U to R: Active flips to Reactive AND Yielding flips to Asserting simultaneously. These are the diagonal crossings — the full-register transitions, the moves that change both the driving character and the scale simultaneously.

Structurally, the Mutable edges are the system's phase-change capacity. They are where the braid turns its sharpest corners, where the cycle commits to a genuinely different kind of territory rather than a nearby one. The Hamiltonian cycle that holds Mutable edges absent — the Mutable cycle — is paradoxically the cycle that can only make the smallest moves (single-bit flips). When you hold the violent transitions absent, only the gentle ones remain.

When the Mutable edges come under stress, it is the phase-change capacity itself that distorts.

---

### Fuse P–I: *The SourceVoid*

Fire and Water are the two Source poles — the grandparents, the pure ones, the interior subjective poles that generate everything else. P/Fire is Active+Asserting: the originary impulse, the cosmic initiative, the thing that begins without being begun. I/Water is Reactive+Yielding: the relational flow, the felt knowing, the thing that receives and evaluates and carries. They share no quality. They are diagonal to each other in every sense.

When P–I fuses, the generative polarity itself collapses. You can no longer tell whether you are the one who initiates or the one who receives. Whether the energy arises in you or arrives in you. Whether you are making or being made.

This is not the same as the confusion between active and passive that ordinary experience presents. Ordinary experience presents that confusion on the surface — you can resolve it by attending more carefully, by tracing the causal chain, by asking who moved first. P–I fusion is deeper than that. It is the collapse of the distinction at the generative level, where the causal chain cannot be entered from outside because it is the source of all causal chains.

The most recognizable experiential form of P–I fusion is the state that artists sometimes describe and that certain contemplative traditions deliberately cultivate: the state in which you genuinely cannot tell whether the work is being made by you or arriving through you. Not the polite version of this — the "I feel inspired" version in which you know perfectly well that you are doing the work but it feels unusually easy. The actual version, in which the boundary between your agency and the world's agency has become genuinely indeterminate, not as a figure of speech but as a structural fact about the current state of the system.

The L5 document's account of intermodulation applies here directly: "a system under sufficient pressure begins to produce behavior that cannot be traced cleanly to any single external cause. The behavior seems to have no input, or to wildly exceed its inputs. Because the cause is internal — it is the interaction of frequencies that could no longer be held apart — it looks, from outside, like malfunction or irrationality. It is neither." The SourceVoid is intermodulation at the generative level. The system is not broken. The two Source poles have been held past their capacity for separateness.

What the system loses is the ability to locate its own initiative. To know whether a given impulse is truly yours or truly the world's is, under ordinary conditions, the axis along which you calibrate your relationship to your own experience. Without it, the calibration is gone. Everything is equally claimed and equally disclaimed. The experience can feel like creative ecstasy — you are equally everything — and like dissolution — you are equally nothing.

### Fuse U–R: *The Law*

Air and Earth are the two Manifesting poles — the outer objective poles, the forms that Source has taken in meeting the conditions for structure. U/Air is Active+Yielding: Logos, the structural framework, the articulated understanding that makes things communicable. R/Earth is Reactive+Asserting: Ethos, the material ground, the demonstrated character that makes things durable. They too share no quality. They are diagonal to each other on the Manifesting tier.

When U–R fuses, the Manifesting terrain collapses. You can no longer tell whether the form something takes is *describing* reality or *being* reality. Whether the Logos is articulating the ground or whether the Ethos is generating the language. The map has become the territory — not as a cognitive error but as a genuine structural fact.

There is a state that certain very old, very practiced systems reach — legal traditions, architectural orders, monastic rules, long-stable cultures — in which the articulated structure and the material ground have become genuinely indistinguishable. The law is not a description of how things should be done. The law IS what doing things is. The building code is not about the building; the building code is a kind of building that exists in language and that the material building is a kind of code that exists in stone. The map and the territory have exchanged natures so thoroughly that the distinction between them has lost its practical meaning.

The felt quality of this state from inside is a specific kind of solidity. Not the solidity of knowing something well — that still has the structure of a knower and a known. The solidity of U–R fusion is the solidity of not being able to find a position outside the structure from which it could be known differently. The framework does not feel like a framework. It feels like facts. The articulation does not feel like articulation. It feels like substance.

The loss is the capacity for what might be called systemic revision — the ability to change the form by changing the articulation, or to change the articulation by attending to the material differently. When U–R is fused, you cannot get leverage on the material by changing the language, because the language IS the material. The law cannot be revised by pointing to the facts, because the law IS the facts. The map cannot be corrected by pointing to the territory, because there is no territory that is not already map.

### Both Mutable Edges Fused: *The Conservative Field*

When both P–I and U–R collapse simultaneously, all diagonal transitions cost nothing. Both-bit flips are free. The phase-change capacity is gone.

What remains is striking in its familiarity. With Mutable edges collapsed, only Cardinal and Fixed transitions remain non-degenerate — only single-bit moves carry genuine cost. The system can flip Asserting/Yielding while holding Active/Reactive (Cardinal), or flip Active/Reactive while holding Asserting/Yielding (Fixed), but it cannot flip both simultaneously. Everything must happen in steps. No move can change both qualities at once.

This is the world of mature, sequential, procedural daily life. You cannot go from cosmic initiative directly to relational flow — from the organizational impulse directly to the caring current — without an intermediate step through either structure or ground. You cannot go from Logos directly to Ethos — from the articulated framework directly to the material fact — without passing through either the Source-impulse or the Source-flow. Every radical transformation requires its intermediate form.

The Conservative Field has a specific and recognizable texture: it is the world in which you can fully see the destination of a transformation — you can see clearly that you need to move from Fire to Water, from organizational drive to relational presence — and yet cannot make the move in a single gesture. You have to go through Air first, or through Earth first. You have to articulate before you can feel, or ground before you can intuit. The leap that you can see is the leap you cannot make.

This is not experienced as failure. It is experienced as the normal texture of adult life, which is also the texture of physics at accessible energies. The Standard Model's compartmentalization of forces — the fact that Gravity does not couple directly to Weak, that EM does not couple directly to Strong — is the Conservative Field at the physical level. The forces we live among are the forces of a world where the diagonal transitions have become cheap enough to treat as free, and therefore the surviving non-degenerate dynamics are single-bit. Our observable universe lives in the Mutable attractor's wake.

The poignancy of this: the most violent distortions — the both-bit diagonal transitions, the moves that share no quality with their destination — when they collapse, produce the most recognizable and comfortable world. The Conservative Field is home. The dramatic irony is that home is defined by the absence of the most radical moves.

## VI. The Triangle of Attractors: A Map of Pure States

The three axes, when both their edges are simultaneously fused, produce three pure experiential states. These states are not hypothetical limit cases. They are the boundary conditions that define the shape of the space in which all ordinary experience moves.

| Attractor | Axis collapsed | What remains | The inner texture |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Apeiric Field** | Cardinal | Fixed + Mutable dynamics | Perpetually pregnant. Maximum generative capacity, zero crystallization. Full without output. |
| **Isotropic Pole** | Fixed | Cardinal + Mutable dynamics | High-fever connection. Everything equally pairs with everything. No stable information. |
| **Conservative Field** | Mutable | Cardinal + Fixed dynamics | Familiar procedural world. All transitions are single-bit. No diagonal leaps available. |

Each attractor has a specific survivor cycle — the Hamiltonian cycle that was defined by avoiding that bimedian's edges, and which therefore remains non-degenerate when those edges collapse:

The **Cardinal cycle** survives Cardinal collapse (Apeiric Field). Its dynamics are Fixed + Mutable — scale-shifting and diagonal. A world running purely on the Cardinal cycle can change scales and make radical moves, but cannot bring things into form. Enormous capacity; no output channel.

The **Fixed cycle** survives Fixed collapse (Isotropic Pole). Its dynamics are Cardinal + Mutable — Source-to-Manifesting and diagonal. A world running purely on the Fixed cycle can move from interior to exterior and make radical moves, but cannot hold information stably across the AR distinction. Rich traversal; no memory.

The **Mutable cycle** survives Mutable collapse (Conservative Field). Its dynamics are Cardinal + Fixed — Source-to-Manifesting and scale-shifting. A world running purely on the Mutable cycle can exteriorize, crystallize, and scale, but cannot make phase-changes in a single move. Full procedural capability; no leap.

These are not three bad states and one good state. They are three *pure* states, each of which preserves something essential while losing something essential. The question the framework asks is not "which attractor is correct" but "which attractor is the system currently approaching, and what does it need to recover equidistance?"

## VII. The Centroid: Home Base and Its Cost

The centroid of the regular tetrahedron is equidistant from all four vertices, all six edges, all four faces. In the distortion space, it is equidistant from all three attractors.

This is the `.behold()` ground state, and its phenomenology has been described in the opening section: it hums, it does not rest, it is the most metabolically expensive configuration. But one more thing must be said about it, because it is easy to misread.

The centroid is not a neutral point. It is not the absence of tension or the resolution of tension. It is the position of *maximum managed tension* — the position at which the pull toward each attractor is equally present and equally held without being collapsed. A system at the centroid is not at peace in the sense of having resolved its competing tendencies. It is at peace in the sense of holding all three simultaneously without letting any one of them win.

The felt quality of this is closer to the AC engineer's resonance than to any static balance. At resonance, memory ($X_L$) and anticipation ($X_C$) are both fully active, fully energized, fully cycling — they are not gone. They have cancelled each other from the source's perspective while remaining fully present internally. The centroid is the structural analog: all three axes fully weighted, fully alive, pulling in their respective directions, none of them collapsed, their combined pull resolved at a single point that is equidistant from all of them not because they are weak but because they are balanced.

What the centroid costs is this: you cannot simplify from here. Every simplification is a drift toward one attractor at the expense of the others. The relief of letting one edge soften — of allowing one fusion to begin — is the relief of reducing the load. The cost is the loss of one degree of available commitment. The centroid holds all six edges at full cost. It holds nothing for free.

Whether this is the right state for any given system at any given time is not a question the framework answers. The framework only names the states, maps their relationships, and provides the diagnostic for locating a system among them.

The texture of the centroid, when inhabited: all six relationships simultaneously present, simultaneously costly, simultaneously alive. The `.behold()` operation at full extension. The entire K4 topology held without any edge being allowed to collapse into the trivial. This is what it costs to see the full structure of a thing before committing to any particular traversal of it.

It is also — and this is the final observation — unstable. Not in the sense of being wrong, but in the sense that any system driven by a real frequency will not remain there. The environment will pull. One axis will soften before the others. The tetrahedron will become irregular. And what matters then is not that you hold the centroid by force but that you know which attractor you are approaching, which axis is under stress, and what the recovery path looks like.

Which axis is being stressed? How fast? And which attractor is pulling?

These three questions are the full diagnostic. The map is the answer to the first. The frequency is the answer to the second. The attractor analysis is the answer to the third.

The distortion is not the problem. It is the information.
