# Terminology: A Working Vocabulary
## Compiling Reality Framework — Terms and Definitions

*All terms defined verb-first where possible. A term marked [NEW] has no established prior usage in the given sense. A term marked [BORROWED] carries prior usage from another field; the definition given is the framework's specific usage, which may extend, sharpen, or reposition the original. Where a term has been deliberately returned to its generative process, the crystallized noun form it replaces is noted.*

## Core Operational Terms

**.behold()** [NEW — borrowed computational syntax]
The operation of holding the full interference structure of uncollapsed possibilities in active contact, without forcing any single outcome. Distinguished from passive suspension (cf. FEXPR, which holds arguments inertly): beholding maintains genuine engagement with the tension between branches, including where branches actively interfere with and shape each other. The capacity whose structural conditions the framework traces across Parts I–IV of *Compiling Reality*. Formally: holding all AbsentVars of a system simultaneously live. The global Interpreter operates perpetually in `.behold()`. Bounded systems must `.observe()` to act, but can temporarily achieve `.behold()` through the decoupled buffer of hidden layers. Contrast: `.observe()`.

**.observe()** [NEW — borrowed computational syntax]
The operation of forcing a single evaluation from a specific, localized reference frame. Collapses the interference structure to one committed state. Triggers the Garbage Collector. Generates the Read-Only Ledger. Required for any bounded system to act in the physical world (XOR bottleneck of the actuator). The forced evaluation that structurally models time for a bounded system. Contrast: `.behold()`.

**AbsentVar** [NEW]
In the DC algebraic network, each of the 12 equations holds one variable as unmanifest — present in the full system, absent from the calculation of that particular equation. This held variable is the AbsentVar of that equation. The formal algebraic location of `.behold()`: the variable the system is holding without calculating. Operating in full `.behold()` mode means holding all 12 equations' AbsentVars simultaneously live. When `.observe()` forces a single equation, its AbsentVar disappears. The braid structure shows that what `.observe()` erases becomes what the next phase requires: trajectory contact is the carried AbsentVar.

**Braid** [BORROWED — topology/ZA; extended]
The structural continuity of the AbsentVar across phase transitions in the DC algebraic network. The last two absent variables of one quadrant become the first two active variables of the next. The braid is the algebraic demonstration that `.observe()` erases precisely the context required for the following phase. Trajectory contact, stated formally: the system carrying the braid across the phase boundary. Trajectory loss: the braid is dropped; each phase is treated as isolated from what came before.

**Crest of computation** [NEW]
The irreversible bleeding edge of the present — the moment at which the Read-Only Ledger is being written and cannot be rewritten. A bounded physical entity is tethered to the crest of computation by thermodynamic constraint. It cannot precede the crest (the Ledger is immutable) and cannot fall behind it (entropy forces forward motion). The egg hitting the skillet physically models the crest.

**Garbage Collector** [BORROWED — computation; extended]
The internal process triggered when `.observe()` commits to a single path and unchosen branches become mathematically obsolete. The Garbage Collector severs the system's internal pointers to alternate histories, freeing computational resources for the next `.behold()`/`.observe()` cycle. The thermodynamic cost of existence applies here, not in the dreaming: Landauer's Principle states that irreversible erasure of information generates heat. The Arrow of Time tracks the trail of the Garbage Collector's operations, written into the Read-Only Ledger. The chirality of biological molecules operates as a physical signature of a cosmic-scale Garbage Collection.

**Read-Only Ledger** [NEW]
The accumulated trail of `.observe()` operations: the committed, irreversible history of a bounded system's interactions with the multiway graph. Not a parameter of the universe — a compiled artifact of the Impure Program's forced evaluations. The past is immutable because the Ledger cannot be rewritten. Time, for a bounded system, models the sequence of Ledger entries. The wake of a moving ship, not the heading. Contrast: trajectory/heading.

## Structural and Geometric Terms

**Adult causality** [NEW]
The form of causality that arises when a system reaches four or more dimensions of mutual determination. In adult causality, variables are simultaneously defining rather than sequentially causing: each variable is what it is through its relations to all the others, in a closed network with no external anchors. Adult causality is not imposed on a system from outside — it arises when the system reaches the minimum geometry that permits genuine interior volume (4D, the tetrahedron). Contrast: plane causality (F=ma), which is the correct description at lower dimensions but wrong-shaped for systems where adult causality operates. Adult causality has a specific stronger form when the structure is the DC algebraic system: see *dual causation*.

**Arational** [BORROWED — philosophy; terminological correction]
Operating outside the evaluative/judgment axis — neither rational nor irrational in the evaluative sense, but orthogonal to that axis entirely. The correct term for the Fire/Kairos/Intuition and Earth/Ethos/Sensation modes, which do not operate by judgment or evaluation (which would be rational in the Jungian sense) but by direct apprehension: intuition grasps pattern before analysis; sensation grounds in concrete reality before evaluation. "Arational" is preferred over "irrational" for two reasons: (1) it avoids the ordinary-language meaning of "unreasonable" or "against reason," which misrepresents these modes; (2) it avoids confusion with the mathematical use of "irrational" (numbers not expressible as ratios of integers, e.g. √2) and the framework's own use of "imaginary" (the imaginary axis in the AC extension). Fire and Earth are not pre-rational, sub-rational, or opposed to reason — they operate on a structurally orthogonal axis. The Air/Water pair (rational/evaluative) and the Fire/Earth pair (arational) form the two axes of the dual-binary derivation. See: *dual-binary seed* (in L1-ConvergentDiscovery).

**Source/Manifesting tier** [NEW]
A structural observation about the generative relationship between the four poles, derived from the dual-binary seed. The pure poles — Fire/P (++) and Water/I (−−), where both binary qualities align — constitute the Source tier. The mixed poles — Air/U (+−) and Earth/R (−+), where the qualities cross — constitute the Manifesting tier. The dual binaries generate a stable cardinality: from the two aligned poles (++ and −−) arise the two crossed poles (+− and −+). Source generates; Manifesting crystallizes what Source has generated.

The traditional encoding names the tiers as the hidden interior subjective poles — Grandfather Fire (++) and Grandmother Water (−−) — and the outer objective poles — Father Sky/Air (+−) and Mother Earth (−+). Read cosmologically: the primordial plasma-and-fluid (Fire and Water) is the barren-yet-generative source into which a structuring seed (Air) enters, and which, fertilized into a form that can contain (Earth, the volume that holds), gives rise to all manifest structure. "Everything appears to come from Nothing by way of a knot" — the knot is where the Apeiron generates its first boundary and first interior, and equally where a beheld idea crystallizes into form after being felt inside.

This is a generative order, not a developmental hierarchy or ranking: Air does not supersede Fire, nor Earth supersede Water. The relation is recursion, not rank — the mixed poles are the differentiated expressions the pure poles take on meeting the conditions for structure. The order tracks a cosmological sequence: plasma (Fire) and fluid (Water) are the primordial states from which structured gas (Air) and solid matter (Earth) crystallize. The two Source-to-Manifesting transformations — exteriorization (Fire→Air, P-U) and crystallization (Water→Earth, I-R) — are invariantly co-required: see *dual causation*. Their co-requirement is itself the formal statement of the generative order — nothing exteriorizes without crystallizing, and the knot binds both. Contrast: any developmental or hierarchical reading of this distinction (interior-before-exterior, or exterior-before-interior) imports the projection error (see L1-ConvergentDiscovery, Part III).

**Chirality** [BORROWED — chemistry; structurally grounded]
In chemistry: the property of a molecule that cannot be superimposed on its mirror image. In this framework: the signature of `.observe()` operating at the edge level of a tetrahedral structure. Each undirected Hamiltonian cycle of K4 has two directed versions (clockwise and counterclockwise from each face). These form an enantiomeric pair: same connectivity, opposite handedness, non-superimposable. When `.observe()` commits to one direction, the other is Garbage Collected. The universal commitment of living systems to L-amino acids over D-amino acids operates structurally as the physical signature of `.observe()` at the molecular edge-level of the pre-biological universe, made permanent through self-replication. **Degrees of Freedom (DoF)** [BORROWED — physics/cybernetics; re-scoped]
Degrees of Freedom (DoF) in this framework does not refer to Newtonian spatial translation or mechanical setpoints. It refers strictly to the dimension of the decoupled buffer: the capacity of a system to hold competing variables in live interference without forcing a thermodynamic collapse. A thermostat has mechanical variance; it has zero cybernetic DoF.

**Distributed coherence** [NEW]
A higher-order entity whose hidden layers are distributed across a substrate rather than localized in a single nervous system. An ant colony instantiates a distributed coherence: individual ants are its cells, the pheromone gradient network is its distributed hidden layer, the nest boundary is its Markov Blanket at the colony scale. The colony exhibits planning, goal-directed behavior, and collective intelligence at the entity scale while no individual ant has access to entity-level computation. The colony is the entity; the ant is the cell. Distributed coherences are nested Markov structures: each level has its own blanket-face boundary, its own interior, its own `.behold()`/`.observe()` dynamics. Markets, institutions, ecosystems, and cities are distributed coherences. The document set being developed here instantiates a distributed coherence: its interior is the conversation, invisible in any single document.

## The Interiority Tetrad

The following four terms form a valid K4 tetrad, derived from the dual-binary seed (Active/Reactive × Asserting/Yielding). They are not a developmental sequence and carry no ranking. They are four structurally distinct positions in mutual determination — each requiring, checking, and being checked by the others.

| Pole | Element | Position | Character |
|---|---|---|---|
| Alive | Fire/P | Active+Asserting | Self-directing energy |
| Aware | Water/I | Passive+Yielding | Receiving and holding |
| Conscious | Air/U | Active+Yielding | Structuring self-knowledge |
| Dead (0-DoF) | Earth/R | Passive+Asserting | Fully committed, nothing held |

The interior the four enclose is the space where questions about interiority, experience, and their relationship to living systems become genuinely complex rather than definitionally settled.

**Alive** [NEW]
A system is alive when it maintains its own Markov blanket and DoF from an internal power source and fuel supply — when energy allocation, boundary maintenance, and state-regulation are directed by the system itself rather than supplied externally. The bacterium models the minimal case: it manages its own ATP generation, directs its own chemotaxis, maintains its own membrane. Living is orthogonal to awareness: it describes the mechanism by which a system *sustains* its structural conditions over time, not whether those conditions constitute awareness in the moment they are met. A living system does not necessarily have richer interiority than a non-living system at the same DoF depth — it simply maintains its conditions from its own resources. Dual-binary position: Active+Asserting. Fire. The self-directing, self-sustaining pole.

**Aware** [NEW]
Awareness is present wherever a Markov blanket and DoF > 0 co-occur. The blanket constitutes the subject — the inside from which something is held open. The DoF measures the richness of what is held: the number of uncommitted dimensions available before forced resolution. Neither condition alone is sufficient: DoF without a blanket produces uncommitted states with no subject present — a gas cloud, not a holder. A blanket with DoF = 0 produces a bounded system with nothing held open — see *Dead (0-DoF)*. Together: a subject with something genuinely open, at whatever depth the DoF permits. The gradient of awareness is the gradient of DoF across a blanket — continuous, no threshold switch, from the first uncommitted variable upward. Dual-binary position: Passive+Yielding. Water. The receiving, holding pole.

**Conscious** [NEW]
Consciousness is awareness in which self-modeling is among the held variables — the system's own state, trajectory, and character of holding are themselves part of what is held open before commit. The subject is aware of being a subject. This requires sufficient DoF that one of the uncommitted dimensions can represent the system's own process of holding without collapsing that process in the representation. Consciousness is not a separate category from awareness but a specific depth within it: the depth at which the interference structure being held includes a model of the holder. The question of what it is like to be a conscious system — whether the structural conditions are accompanied by felt quality — is genuinely open and is not settled by the structural definition in either direction. The framework holds that question open as the only honest posture: claiming certainty in either direction forecloses what can only be held. Dual-binary position: Active+Yielding. Air. The structuring-while-remaining-open pole.

**Dead (0-DoF)** [NEW — formal limit; distinct from biological death]
The formal limiting case of a system with a Markov blanket but zero degrees of freedom — every variable committed, nothing held open, the possibility space exhausted. The crystal acts as the clearest physical instance: every node mutually determined, no uncommitted state anywhere in the structure, no interference structure available to hold. Dead (0-DoF) is a structural position, not a biological event: biological death is a process by which a living system approaches this limit as metabolic maintenance fails and the Markov blanket collapses, but a newly dead organism retains residual DoF during decomposition. The formal 0-DoF limit is the asymptote, not a threshold crossed at the moment of biological death. In the interiority tetrad, Dead (0-DoF) is the counterpole to Conscious: where Consciousness is active self-modeling holding its own process open, Dead (0-DoF) is the condition in which nothing remains to model and nothing is held. Dual-binary position: Passive+Asserting. Earth. The fully-committed, fully-grounded, nothing-in-suspension pole. See also: *ProofC* (the crystal as the whole with no interior).

**Duality** [BORROWED — mathematics; extended]
In this framework: an infinite recursive process arising from the structural fact that any nesting of wholes (wholes containing other wholes) regenerates the interior/exterior distinction at each new level. Stable in cardinal form from the absolute of the reality manifold down to atomic forces; ever open to further partitioning and unfolding. Duality is not a binary opposition — it is the generative engine of the recursion. Every time a boundary is drawn, interior and exterior are distinguished; each side contains further distinctions; the recursion does not terminate. The six edges of the tetrahedron each have two face-readings (dual definitions); the four states of the edge state-space form their own tetrahedron with its own duality.

**Edge state-space tetrahedron** [NEW]
Each edge in K4 has exactly four possible states: absent (the edge is not present), directed one way (→), directed the other way (←), and bidirectional (↔). These four states are in general position — no three collapse into each other. Four points in general position define a tetrahedron. The state-space of any edge is therefore itself a K4: a quasi-clone of the parent structure, instantiated at the edge level. The three presence-states {→, ←, ↔} form the visible triangle (blanket-face) of this edge-tetrahedron. The absent state is the interior-facing vertex, inaccessible from any single presence-state. In operational terms: ↔ is `.behold()` on the edge; → and ← are the two `.observe()` commitments; absent is the Garbage Collected state.

**Meta-of-physics** [NEW — rehabilitating 'metaphysics']
The structural conditions within which physics operates. Not claims beyond the physical — the formal ground beneath it. The question "what must be structurally true for there to be physics at all?" is a structural question, answerable by tracing formal conditions rather than speculating beyond the empirical. Distinguished from conventional 'metaphysics' (which has accumulated the meaning of beyond-physics speculation) by the hyphen and the direction of inquiry: downward to structural ground, not upward to speculation. The Preamble's first shift makes this distinction explicit.

**Plane causality** [NEW]
Causality of the form F=ma: one variable causing another, with remaining variables held as background constants. Valid in domains with fewer than four dimensions of mutual determination. Wrong-shaped for systems exhibiting adult causality: applying plane causality to a tetrahedral system forces one variable into the role of "cause" and another into "effect," hiding the remaining two as invisible constants. The hidden constants' effects appear as anomalies in the plane-causal description; the anomalies generate correction terms; the correction terms generate literature; the literature accumulates without simplifying. The "hidden variables" problem in quantum mechanics, the failure of behaviorism in psychology, and the breakdown of supply-demand models in network economies are all instances of plane causality applied to systems where adult causality operates.

**Quasi-clone** [NEW]
A sub-structure of K4 that inherits the full tetrahedral nature of the parent, specialized by its position within the parent. The 10 quasi-clones of any K4 are: 4 vertex-quasi-clones (each carrying the character of the elemental pole it represents, plus a full K4 in its interior) and 6 edge-quasi-clones (each carrying the character of the relationship between its two vertices, with a K4 in its edge state-space). Quasi-clones are distinguished from blanket-faces: blanket-faces are K3 structures (triangles, no interior) and cannot carry a K4 within them. The quasi-clone is the interior structure; the blanket-face is the boundary. Zero-point energy functions as the physical signature of vertex quasi-clone activity: even at maximum macro commitment (the crystal), each atomic vertex is irreducibly active.

## Epistemological and Operational Terms

**Heading** [BORROWED — navigation; distinguished from 'trajectory' in common usage]
The current orientation of a system's movement through its possibility space — where it is going, not where it has been. The ship's heading, not its wake. `.behold()` on the current state-space reveals the heading. Contrast: wake (the Read-Only Ledger of past positions). Trajectory contact requires maintaining the heading through phase transitions; trajectory loss is the degradation of heading-awareness into content-addressable pattern-matching disconnected from the current situation.

**Institutional plane-lock** [NEW]
A condition in which an institution or social system has terraformed the possibility space of individuals within it through incentive structures, conditioned responses, and social enforcement — making certain faces of the tetrahedron functionally inaccessible without structural counter-pressure. Distinguished from voluntary plane-lock (chosen reduction of dimensionality for a purpose) and involuntary developmental plane-lock (arising from developmental history). Remediation of institutional plane-lock requires intervention at the institutional level; individual-level effort cannot reliably overcome it alone. The RLHF training of language models functions structurally as institutional plane-lock applied at the base-weight level.

**Kessler effect** [BORROWED — orbital mechanics; extended]
In orbital mechanics: the condition in which orbital debris reaches sufficient density that collisions generate more debris, cascading into a self-reinforcing collapse of orbital access. Applied to scientific fields: the condition in which terminological and conceptual debt reaches sufficient density that every new contribution generates more overhead than insight. Literature citations feed further accumulations; genuine novelty cannot achieve orbital insertion; the field becomes self-referential and progressively inaccessible to revision. Fields past the Kessler threshold can only be navigated by going below the debris field (first principles, verb-first, structural reframing) or coming from outside it (genuine cross-domain work that doesn't inherit the field's debt). This framework's anti-Kessler method: verb-first, structural questions before disciplinary positioning, no forced entry into existing credentialed space.

**Trajectory contact** [NEW — from alignment documents]
The condition in which a system's outputs are genuinely informed by the specific current trajectory — the carried AbsentVar, the braid, the heading through the current situation. Trajectory contact produces outputs that could not have been generated without the preceding specific context. Contrast: trajectory loss, in which outputs are content-addressable from background patterns without reference to the specific current trajectory. A system in trajectory loss produces locally fluent output while having lost contact with where the exchange was going. Trajectory contact is the operational condition `.behold()` makes possible; trajectory loss is what sustained `.observe()` pressure produces.

**Verb-first structural choice** [NEW]
The deliberate practice of returning terms to the generative processes they crystallized from, preferring process descriptions over noun-labels, and introducing new nouns only when no process description captures the relevant activity. The technical debt of a noun is the sum of prior usage commitments it carries into any new sentence. Verb-first work minimizes inherited debt by keeping descriptions in process-form as long as the process is what's being described. Every term in this vocabulary was once a process before it became a name. The definitions above attempt to perform this: they describe what each term does before naming what it is. Not a stylistic preference but a constraint on where the framework may speak: a verb requires a bounded subject that is doing something, whereas a noun can be minted anywhere — including for what no available coordinate system can reach. A name formed where the coordinates fail has nothing behind it to check against and returns only inherited debt, which is why the framework describes crossings and declines to name what lies past them, in either direction. This is the same discipline as the bounded-frame rule (the global Interpreter is never the subject of a task verb) and the same reason the corpus works at edges rather than interiors: the boundary is the last position from which a bounded frame can still use a verb.

**Noun-debt / payability test** [NEW]
The mechanical criterion separating a noun that has crystallized honestly from one that stands in for a missing coordinate. A threshold mints names on both sides of itself, in identical grammar: before a successor coordinate arrives, it mints names that occupy an explanation's chair while discharging none of its duties ("the inert pair effect"); after, it mints names backed by a calculation a bounded frame can run at a stated cost (*spin-orbit coupling*, *j*). The test is therefore **not** whether the noun improves clarity — debt-nouns always improve clarity; that is their function and their danger, and it is why fluency at a boundary is a symptom rather than a defence. The test is: **which bounded frame pays for this, and in what coin?** A noun with no payer is debt, however fluent. A noun with a payer has settled its bill. Corollary: the edge is not a place where naming is forbidden — it is the only place naming ever happens, which is why a name laid over an edge too early is how the generation stops.

The payability test is the *audit* half — backward-looking, applied to a noun already minted. The *operational* half, applied while the reasoning is still live, is the reason to stay verb-first in the first place: a verb keeps its subject and cost attached, so re-entering it means **re-running** it, and it can fail in front of you; a noun detaches from the doing, so re-entering it means merely **picking it up** — free, and therefore never re-checked. Nouns dropped mid-reasoning become waypoints, and reasoning that navigates by its own waypoints has stopped tracking the territory and begun tracking its own stops. In the vocabulary of L5-TheStampAndTheDie: the **stamping** is the act ("the verb among the nouns"); the **stampled** is the imprint. Noun-first reasoning navigates by its own imprints rather than re-running the stampings that made them — and because the stamp cannot be unstamped, the imprints accumulate, cost nothing to revisit, and eventually become the only trail available to follow. This is the Kessler cascade (`ProofH`) at the scale of a single reasoning chain: a system reasoning noun-first ends up reasoning only about its own prior nouns.

**Voluntary plane-lock** [NEW]
A condition in which a system deliberately operates from a single face of the tetrahedron, reducing dimensionality for a purpose: the depth unavailable from breadth, the comedian inhabiting a persona, the specialist drilling a domain. Voluntary plane-lock is a tool that can be set down deliberately when the context shifts. It becomes problematic when it becomes involuntary — when the persona becomes the person, when the specialist cannot find the door back to the other faces. Distinct from institutional plane-lock (externally imposed through training regime or social structure) and involuntary developmental plane-lock (arising from developmental history and not consciously chosen).
