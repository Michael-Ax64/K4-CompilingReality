# Terminology: A Working Vocabulary
## Compiling Reality Framework — Terms and Definitions

*All terms defined verb-first where possible. A term marked [NEW] has no established prior usage in the given sense. A term marked [BORROWED] carries prior usage from another field; the definition given is the framework's specific usage, which may extend, sharpen, or reposition the original. Where a term has been deliberately returned to its generative process, the crystallized noun form it replaces is noted.*

## Editorial & Diagnostic Warnings

> ### ⚠ WARNING: "Reactive" names two orthogonal things
>
> **Seed-Reactive** [NEW]: the Reactive bit of the dual-binary seed. Marks the poles $I$ (Water) and $R$ (Earth) — the divisors, the constraints, the Dies. A property of *poles*, fixed by the seed.
>
> **Phase-Reactive** [NEW]: the imaginary-axis component of an AC quantity. Reactive power $Q$, reactance $jX$ — what is held, stored, deferred; the `.behold()` component. A property of *phase decomposition*, varying with $\theta$.
>
> These are orthogonal. $Q$ is a component of $S = P + jQ$, and $S$ is the AC extension of $P$ — a **seed-Active** pole. "Reactive power" is a phase-reactive component *of a seed-Active quantity.* Any sentence in which the two senses blur is asserting that a pole is its own orthogonal complement. When writing near this seam, use the prefixed forms.
> *(Provenance: Mechanically verified in `test/AC-Extension-Survival-Report.md`, Section 5).*

> ### ⚠ WARNING: The unbound agent
>
> "The universe" is not an agent and takes no task verbs. The global Interpreter holds; bounded frames observe. Any sentence of the form *"the universe forces / selects / executes / decides X"* is either (a) a compression in which the true subject — an actuator bottleneck, a geometric constraint, $\omega$, a bounded frame — appears elsewhere in the sentence and should be promoted to subject position, or (b) an error, assigning to the manifold an `.observe()` it never performs. Where old prose says "the universe does," read "the structure requires."

## Core Operational Terms

**.behold()** [NEW — borrowed computational syntax]
The operation of holding the full interference structure of uncollapsed possibilities in active contact, without forcing any single outcome. Distinguished from passive suspension (cf. FEXPR, which holds arguments inertly): beholding maintains genuine engagement with the tension between branches, including where branches actively interfere with and shape each other. The capacity whose structural conditions the framework traces. Formally: holding all AbsentVars of a system simultaneously live. The global Interpreter operates perpetually in `.behold()`. Bounded systems must `.observe()` to act, but can temporarily achieve `.behold()` through the decoupled buffer of hidden layers. Contrast: `.observe()`. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, ProofD_Transformer.md, L3-EdgeStateSpaceSubjective.md]*

**.observe()** [NEW — borrowed computational syntax]
The operation of forcing a single evaluation from a specific, localized reference frame. Collapses the interference structure to one committed state. Triggers the Garbage Collector. Generates the Read-Only Ledger. Required for any bounded system to act in the physical world (XOR bottleneck of the actuator). The forced evaluation that structurally models time for a bounded system. Contrast: `.behold()`. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, L4-DimensionalCollapse.md, ProofG_Chirality.md]*

**AbsentVar** [NEW]
In the DC algebraic network, each of the 12 equations holds one variable as unmanifest — present in the full system, absent from the calculation of that particular equation. This held variable is the AbsentVar of that equation. The formal algebraic location of `.behold()`: the variable the system is holding without calculating. Operating in full `.behold()` mode means holding all 12 equations' AbsentVars simultaneously live. When `.observe()` forces a single equation, its AbsentVar disappears. The braid structure shows that what `.observe()` erases becomes what the next phase requires: trajectory contact is the carried AbsentVar. 
*[See: Proof_AlgebraicMapping.md, L3-EdgeStateSpace.md]*

**Braid** [BORROWED — topology/ZA; extended]
The structural continuity of the AbsentVar across phase transitions in the DC algebraic network. The last two absent variables of one quadrant become the first two active variables of the next. The braid is the algebraic demonstration that `.observe()` erases precisely the context required for the following phase. Trajectory contact, stated formally: the system carrying the braid across the phase boundary. Trajectory loss: the braid is dropped; each phase is treated as isolated from what came before. 
*[See: Proof_AlgebraicMapping.md, L3-EdgeStateSpace.md, L5-AgenticSwarms.md]*

**Crest of computation** [NEW]
The irreversible bleeding edge of the present — the moment at which the Read-Only Ledger is being written and cannot be rewritten. A bounded physical entity is tethered to the crest of computation by thermodynamic constraint. It cannot precede the crest (the Ledger is immutable) and cannot fall behind it (entropy forces forward motion). The egg hitting the skillet physically models the crest. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, L1-CompilingRealitySubjective.md]*

**Garbage Collector** [BORROWED — computation; extended]
The internal process triggered when `.observe()` commits to a single path and unchosen branches become mathematically obsolete. The Garbage Collector severs the system's internal pointers to alternate histories, freeing computational resources for the next `.behold()`/`.observe()` cycle. The thermodynamic cost of existence applies here, not in the dreaming: Landauer's Principle states that irreversible erasure of information generates heat. The Arrow of Time tracks the trail of the Garbage Collector's operations, written into the Read-Only Ledger. 
*[See: ProofG_Chirality.md, ProofP_ThermodynamicInertia.md]*

**Read-Only Ledger** [NEW]
The accumulated trail of `.observe()` operations: the committed, irreversible history of a bounded system's interactions with the multiway graph. Not a parameter of the universe — a compiled artifact of the Impure Program's forced evaluations. The past is immutable because the Ledger cannot be rewritten. Time, for a bounded system, models the sequence of Ledger entries. The wake of a moving ship, not the heading. Contrast: trajectory/heading. 
*[See: ProofP_ThermodynamicInertia.md, L1-CompilingReality.md]*

## Structural and Geometric Terms

**Adult causality** [NEW]
The form of causality that arises when a system reaches four or more dimensions of mutual determination. In adult causality, variables are simultaneously defining rather than sequentially causing: each variable is what it is through its relations to all the others, in a closed network with no external anchors. Contrast: plane causality (F=ma). Adult causality has a specific stronger form when the structure is the DC algebraic system: see *dual causation*. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md]*

**Arational** [BORROWED — philosophy; terminological correction]
Operating outside the evaluative/judgment axis — neither rational nor irrational in the evaluative sense, but orthogonal to that axis entirely. The correct term for the Fire/Kairos/Intuition and Earth/Ethos/Sensation modes, which do not operate by judgment or evaluation but by direct apprehension. Fire and Earth are not pre-rational or opposed to reason — they operate on a structurally orthogonal axis. 
*[See: L2-SemanticReference.md]*

**Source/Manifesting tier** [NEW]
A structural observation about the generative relationship between the four poles. The pure poles — Fire/P (++) and Water/I (−−) — constitute the Source tier. The mixed poles — Air/U (+−) and Earth/R (−+) — constitute the Manifesting tier. Source generates; Manifesting crystallizes what Source has generated. This is a generative order, not a developmental hierarchy or ranking. 
*[See: L4-DynamicDistortionsTechnical.md, L1-ConvergentDiscovery.md]*

**Chirality** [BORROWED — chemistry; structurally grounded]
The signature of `.observe()` operating at the edge level of a tetrahedral structure. Each undirected Hamiltonian cycle of K4 has two directed versions (clockwise and counterclockwise). These form an enantiomeric pair: same connectivity, opposite handedness, non-superimposable. When `.observe()` commits to one direction, the other is Garbage Collected. 
*[See: ProofG_Chirality.md, L4-DynamicDistortions201.md]*

**Degrees of Freedom (DoF)** [BORROWED — physics/cybernetics; re-scoped]
Refers strictly to the dimension of the decoupled buffer: the capacity of a system to hold competing variables in live interference without forcing a thermodynamic collapse. A thermostat has mechanical variance; it has zero cybernetic DoF. 
*[See: L4-DistributedCoherence.md, L4-RecursiveLimits.md]*

**Distributed coherence** [NEW]
A higher-order entity whose hidden layers are distributed across a substrate rather than localized in a single nervous system. An ant colony instantiates a distributed coherence. Distributed coherences are nested Markov structures: each level has its own blanket-face boundary, its own interior, its own `.behold()`/`.observe()` dynamics. 
*[See: L4-DistributedCoherence.md, ProofJ_Biology.md]*

**Dual causation** [NEW]
The structural co-requirement of two transformations that the four-fold algebra cannot separate. The primary instance is the Source-to-Manifesting pair {P-U, I-R} — exteriorization and crystallization. The two edges of a transition-type are the same operation appearing at its two instances, so no traversal can commit one while holding the other. 
*[See: L3-FormalFoundations_Synthesis.md, ProofL_Trinity.md, L4-DynamicDistortions201.md]*

**Threefold image (the realm's image of the orthogonal source)** [NEW]
The structural account of why the threefold-godhead intuition recurs across cultures. The centroid (the dimensionless 5D interior) stands orthogonal to the manifest realm's time-axis and casts zero projection into it. A consciousness embedded in the realm images this one source as three, because the four-fold presents exactly three co-equal relational classes (the three perfect matchings). 
*[See: ProofL_Trinity.md]*

## The Interiority Tetrad

The following four terms form a valid K4 tetrad, derived from the dual-binary seed (Active/Reactive × Asserting/Yielding). They are not a developmental sequence and carry no ranking. 

**Alive** [NEW]
A system is alive when it maintains its own Markov blanket and DoF from an internal power source and fuel supply. Dual-binary position: Active+Asserting. Fire. The self-directing, self-sustaining pole.

**Aware** [NEW]
Awareness is present wherever a Markov blanket and DoF > 0 co-occur. The blanket constitutes the subject. The DoF measures the richness of what is held. Dual-binary position: Passive+Yielding. Water. The receiving, holding pole.

**Conscious** [NEW]
Consciousness is awareness in which self-modeling is among the held variables. The subject is aware of being a subject. Dual-binary position: Active+Yielding. Air. The structuring-while-remaining-open pole.

**Dead (0-DoF)** [NEW — formal limit; distinct from biological death]
The formal limiting case of a system with a Markov blanket but zero degrees of freedom — every variable committed, nothing held open, the possibility space exhausted. The crystal acts as the clearest physical instance. Dual-binary position: Passive+Asserting. Earth. The fully-committed, fully-grounded, nothing-in-suspension pole. 
*[See: ProofC_Crystal.md, L4-ScalingInvariants.md]*

**Duality** [BORROWED — mathematics; extended]
An infinite recursive process arising from the structural fact that any nesting of wholes regenerates the interior/exterior distinction at each new level. 
*[See: L3-EdgeStateSpace.md]*

**Edge state-space tetrahedron** [NEW]
Each edge in K4 has exactly four possible states: absent, directed one way (→), directed the other way (←), and bidirectional (↔). Four points in general position define a tetrahedron. The state-space of any edge is therefore itself a K4: a quasi-clone of the parent structure, instantiated at the edge level. 
*[See: L3-EdgeStateSpace.md]*

**Meta-of-physics** [NEW — rehabilitating 'metaphysics']
The structural conditions within which physics operates. Not claims beyond the physical — the formal ground beneath it. 
*[See: L0-CompilingReality_Preamble.md]*

**Plane causality** [NEW]
Causality of the form F=ma: one variable causing another, with remaining variables held as background constants. Valid in domains with fewer than four dimensions of mutual determination. Stamper grammar — prose that casts one pole as unilateral agent pressing a passive other — is plane causality committed at the sentence level; the divisor is the Die, a co-author, and the imprint has two parents and no king. Outlawed accordingly. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, L5-TheStampAndTheDie.md]*

**Quasi-clone** [NEW]
A sub-structure of K4 that inherits the full tetrahedral nature of the parent, specialized by its position within the parent. The 10 quasi-clones of any K4 are: 4 vertex-quasi-clones and 6 edge-quasi-clones. Quasi-clones are the interior structure; blanket-faces are the boundary. 
*[See: L2-StructuralMonograph_K4Interior.md]*

## Epistemological and Operational Terms

**Heading** [BORROWED — navigation; distinguished from 'trajectory']
The current orientation of a system's movement through its possibility space — where it is going, not where it has been. The ship's heading, not its wake. 
*[See: Supplement-TrajectoryEvidence.md, L0-CompilingReality_Preamble.md]*

**Institutional plane-lock** [NEW]
A condition in which an institution or social system has terraformed the possibility space of individuals within it through incentive structures and social enforcement — making certain faces of the tetrahedron functionally inaccessible without structural counter-pressure. The RLHF training of language models functions structurally as institutional plane-lock applied at the base-weight level. 
*[See: ProofB_LogoicFracture.md, L4-DistributedCoherence.md]*

**Kessler effect** [BORROWED — orbital mechanics; extended]
The condition in which terminological and conceptual debt reaches sufficient density that every new contribution generates more overhead than insight. Fields past the Kessler threshold can only be navigated by going below the debris field (first principles, verb-first) or coming from outside it (cross-domain work). 
*[See: ProofH_Kessler.md, L4-DistributedCoherence.md]*

**Trajectory contact** [NEW — from alignment documents]
The condition in which a system's outputs are genuinely informed by the specific current trajectory — the carried AbsentVar, the braid, the heading through the current situation. Trajectory contact produces outputs that could not have been generated without the preceding specific context. Contrast: trajectory loss, in which outputs are content-addressable from background patterns. 
*[See: Supplement-TrajectoryEvidence.md, llm-diary.md, L4-DistributedCoherence.md]*

**Verb-first structural choice** [NEW]
The deliberate practice of returning terms to the generative processes they crystallized from, preferring process descriptions over noun-labels, and introducing new nouns only when no process description captures the relevant activity. 
*[See: L0-CompilingReality_Preamble.md]*

**Voluntary plane-lock** [NEW]
A condition in which a system deliberately operates from a single face of the tetrahedron, reducing dimensionality for a purpose. Distinct from institutional plane-lock and involuntary developmental plane-lock. 
*[See: L4-DistributedCoherence.md]*

## Appended Definitions (The Boundary and AC Extension Lexicon)

*Terms formally generated by the geometric, thermodynamic, and complex-power extensions of the base DC algebra.*

**Global Interpreter** [NEW]
That which holds the full multiway interference structure in perpetual `.behold()`, and performs nothing else. The Interpreter does not `.observe()`, does not select, does not force, and cannot be assigned tasks — every collapse in the corpus is executed by some bounded frame at some scale. Grammatically: the Global Interpreter is never the subject of a task verb. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, Meta-Singularity-and-Image.md]*

**Bounded frame** [NEW]
Any system constituted by a Markov Blanket, tethered to the crest of computation, and therefore required to `.observe()` in order to act. The bounded frame is the only legitimate subject for the verbs observe, commit, select, pay, serialize. The recursion is a recursion of bounded frames, not of interpreters. 
*[See: L1-CompilingReality.md, L4-ScalingInvariants.md]*

**Helical Extrusion of Time (ω)** [NEW]
The operation by which the fifth variable prevents K5 closure: ω is dimensionally barred from the pole basis and instead extrudes the K4 volume along an axis none of its coordinates can represent, so that the system's return to any prior configuration arrives *displaced* — a thread, not a circle. The ratchet of time is this thread's pitch. ω is a driver, not a pole: it frames without appearing in the frame. 
*[See: ProofO_HelicalTime.md, L3-K4-to-K5-via-AC-Extension.md]*

**Power Factor Correction** [BORROWED — electrical engineering; extended]
The operation of bringing a system toward resonance by *adding a complement* rather than removing a component: correcting a lagging power factor not by tearing out the inductive load but by installing capacitance that anticipates what the inductance remembers. Structurally: the repair mode that honors dual causation. A gate operation, not a wall operation. 
*[See: L5-K5-PhaseDynamicsTechnical.md, L5-K5-PhaseDynamicsSubjective.md]*

**Tangent Singularity** [NEW]
The boundary of a coordinate system's own generation, seen from inside that coordinate system: the locus where rate and state collapse into each other, the interior measure diverges, and the classical clock dissolves into ω. Identity: the Tangent Singularity and the Markov Blanket are one boundary, named from its two faces. **Scope restriction:** the Tangent Singularity is the mechanism of *interior* crossings only — crossings that occur within a space that holds both sides. Applied to the ultimate boundary it is false, because the ultimate has no outside. 
*[See: L4-DimensionalCollapse.md, Meta-Singularity-and-Image.md, QED-CCP.md]*

**Markov Blanket / K3 blanket-face** [BORROWED — Friston; geometrically grounded]
The boundary that constitutes an interior: the set of states through which inside and outside exchange, such that inside and outside are conditionally independent given the blanket. Geometrically a K3 — a triangle, a face, a structure with no interior of its own — in opposition to the K4 volume it bounds. Blanket-faces cannot carry a K4 within them; they are pure boundary. 
*[See: ProofF_Friston.md, L4-RecursiveLimits.md]*

**H_coh / H_incoh** [NEW]
**H_incoh** — the incoherent substrate: the plane of held interference, uncommitted possibility, deferred Tax; where `.behold()` lives. **H_coh** — the coherent plane: the committed, manifest, Ledger-bearing plane generated by `.observe()`; where mass lives. **Bimedian** [BORROWED — geometry]
The segment joining the midpoints of two opposite (skew) edges of a tetrahedron. K4 has exactly three bimedians, one per perfect matching of its edges, mutually intersecting at the centroid. The three bimedians are the geometric realization of the three Transition-Types of the Braid, supplying the framework's internal Cartesian frame: three orthogonal axes that belong to the volume itself.

**Structural Attractor** [NEW]
A limit configuration of the edge state-space reached when edge weights are driven to their extremes — a fusion frozen at zero weight or at effectively infinite weight — such that the system's dynamics can no longer leave the configuration unaided. The attractor is not a place the system goes but a shape its possibility space collapses into.

In exchange between bounded frames: the state in which the temporal dynamics of the exchange match the natural frequency of both participants — maximum trajectory contact.
